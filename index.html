Why Responsive Design Matters

Responsive web design ensures that websites display correctly on all devices, from smartphones to desktop computers.

Flexible layouts improve usability and provide a better experience for users.

Mobile-First Approach

The mobile-first approach focuses on designing for smaller screens first.

As screen size increases, layouts can become more structured and visually balanced.

Quick Tip

Resize your browser window to test responsiveness.
